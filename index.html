The Open Payments Foundation

Financial Infrastructure for the World

The Open Payments Foundation creates open standards and performs technology research in the areas of payments and identity. The foundation is involved in basic research, prototype development, and technology dissemination in the areas of Web-based payment initiation, online identity, credential verification, online trust, and electronic commerce. The mission of the group is to aid in the creation of standards and tools such that every individual in the world may participate in the global financial system free from discrimination against nationality, socioeconomic status, race, gender, religion, political alignment, or other unjust exclusionary practices.
